# JHO Volunteer & Fundraiser Interest Form - Google Form Setup Guide

This guide provides step-by-step instructions for creating a Google Form to collect volunteer and fundraiser interest based on the roles listed on the JHO website.

---

## Getting Started

1. Go to [Google Forms](https://forms.google.com)
2. Click the **+ Blank** button to create a new form
3. Name the form: **"JHO Volunteer & Fundraiser Interest Form"**
4. Add a description:
   > Thank you for your interest in volunteering with JH Outback! Please complete this form and we'll reach out with upcoming opportunities that match your availability and interests.

---

## Section 1: Contact Information

### Question 1: Full Name
- **Type:** Short answer
- **Required:** Yes

### Question 2: Email Address
- **Type:** Short answer
- **Required:** Yes
- **Validation:** Enable "Response validation" → Text → Email

### Question 3: Phone Number
- **Type:** Short answer
- **Required:** Yes
- **Description:** We'll use this to contact you about upcoming events

### Question 4: Preferred Contact Method
- **Type:** Multiple choice
- **Required:** Yes
- **Options:**
  - Email
  - Phone call
  - Text message

---

## Section 2: Volunteer Role Interest

Add a new section with title: **"Volunteer Opportunities"**

Add a description:
> Our events run 1-3 days and offer a variety of volunteer opportunities. Select all roles you're interested in learning more about.

### Question 5: Which volunteer roles interest you?
- **Type:** Checkboxes (allows multiple selections)
- **Required:** Yes
- **Options:**

| Option | Description to add |
|--------|-------------------|
| Event Helper | Perfect for first-time volunteers. Help with setup, registration, activities, meals, and cleanup. Flexible time commitment (half-day to full event). No prior experience required. |
| Activity Leader | Lead specific activities or small groups during events. Guide participants through games, discussions, and team challenges. Requires commitment to full event duration (1-3 days). |
| Event Planner | Work with our core team on logistics, coordination, scheduling, and program development. Involves planning meetings before events and oversight during events. |
| Fundraiser | Help support JHO through fundraising initiatives, donor outreach, grant writing, or event-based fundraising activities. |

## Form Settings

### Configure these settings before publishing:

1. **Responses Tab:**
   - Enable "Collect email addresses" if you want automatic email collection
   - Enable "Send respondents a copy of their response"
   - Set up email notifications for new responses

2. **Settings (gear icon):**
   - **General:**
     - Collect email addresses: Yes
     - Limit to 1 response: Optional (probably leave unchecked)
     - Allow response editing: Yes
   - **Presentation:**
     - Show progress bar: Yes
     - Shuffle question order: No
     - Confirmation message: "Thank you for your interest in volunteering with JH Outback! We'll review your submission and reach out soon with opportunities that match your interests and availability. In the meantime, follow us on social media to stay connected!"

3. **Customize Theme:**
   - Header image: Add JHO logo or event photo
   - Theme color: Use JHO brand blue (#213a86) or teal
   - Font: Keep default or choose a friendly, readable font

---

## After Publishing

1. **Get the shareable link:**
   - Click "Send" button
   - Copy the shortened URL
   - Update the volunteer page link at line 299 of `VolunteersPage.tsx`:
     ```tsx
     href="https://forms.google.com/your-actual-form-link"
     ```

2. **Set up response notifications:**
   - In the Responses tab, click the three-dot menu
   - Select "Get email notifications for new responses"

3. **Create a response spreadsheet:**
   - In the Responses tab, click the green Sheets icon
   - This creates a linked Google Sheet for easier data management

4. **Consider adding integrations:**
   - Connect to a CRM or email marketing tool
   - Set up automatic email responses using Google Forms add-ons

## Tips for Success

- **Test the form** by submitting a test response before sharing publicly
- **Review responses regularly** and follow up within 48 hours
- **Keep the form updated** with current event dates and opportunities
- **Track your volunteers** in a spreadsheet or CRM for ongoing engagement
- **Send confirmation emails** thanking people for their interest
